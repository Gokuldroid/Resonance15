Introduction

Have you ever imagined of controlling a machine/robot using voice Commands ?? Interesting right ?? Yea, here in this workshop on voice controlled robot, you can experience it for yourself.

Components :

  • Arduino
  • A2B cable
  • Robot chassis
  • BO motor
  • Plastic wheels
  • 9v Battery
  • Motor driver shield

Requirements :

A windows laptop in good working conditions(with built in microphone)
