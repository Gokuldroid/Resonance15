Description

If you look outside, multitude of events happen in this world every day. Technologies are growing more and more. But all we need is what?.......Simple 1’s and 0’s. You all heard of the magic word called c10ck. It is the one who is playing in and around the world. This event provides a scrutinizing platform to clock your mind that make you play with digital circuits to have fun.

Rules & Format

  • This is a single round event of duration 45 minutes.
  • It is a team event with maximum of two members.
  • This is a pen and paper event. Use of calculators and other electronic gadgets are not allowed.
  • No negative marking.
  • Decision of the organizers will be final and binding.
