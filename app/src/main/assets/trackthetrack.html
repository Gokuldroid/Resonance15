Introduction

How obedient is your bot?? Dare it cross your line?? This is the event where you track that out.

Problem Statement

Design an autonomous bot that would follow a black line on a white background without losing the line and navigating several turns to reach the destination.

Gameplay

All participants after being checked for the bot constraints will be allowed to let their bots trace the line in the arena. The arena would have several turns which the bot has to manoeuvre properly to reach the destination. You are allowed 3 manual interventions with each one costing you a time penalty. The 4th time drops you out of the game. The top teams which finish the track with the least amount of time are deemed winners and receive exciting prizes.

Rules

  • Each team may have a maximum of 4 members. Lone wolves are welcome too.
  • Participants in the same team can be from different departments.
  • The bot has to be 25x25x25cms in dimension.
