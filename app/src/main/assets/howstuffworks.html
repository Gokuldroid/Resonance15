Description

Questions wake people up. They prompt new ideas. They show people new places, new ways of doing things. So are you someone who loves to ask questions? Do you love finding answers? Are you passionately curious? It’s time to put your curiosity to the test.

Rules & Format

  • A team shall consist of 2 members. Lone wolves are also welcome.
  • The Quiz is for 1 hour and has only one round.
  • The event will consist of 20 questions which can be based on either a picture or a video
  • Questions will be projected on the screen and the answers shall be written on the answer sheet provided.
  • There shall be no negative markings for wrong answers.
  • Hints shall be provided as and when required
  • Use of electronic gadgets is strictly prohibited.
  • The decision of the organizers shall be Final.
