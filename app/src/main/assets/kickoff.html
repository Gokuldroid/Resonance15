Introduction

So you think you’re good at FIFA?? Here is the chance to exhibit some of those skills with the help of your manual rover in winning the Copa de Resonance.

Problem Statement

Design a manual bot which plays soccer i.e. it has to push the ball into the opponent’s goal while defending its own.

Gameplay

The game would be played on a knockout basis. 2(or 3) teams would be allowed to face each other in a match of robot soccer. Each match would be played for about 3(or 5) minutes. The team whose bot successfully scores the most goals will move on to the next stage of knockouts. The winners of the finals would receive exciting prizes. The ball would be size of a ping pong ball or golf ball.

Rules

  • Each team may have a maximum of 4 members. Lone wolves are welcome too.
  • Players in the same team can be from different departments.
  • The bot has to be 25x25x25cms in dimension.
  • The voltage between 2 terminals in the bot should not exceed 12V.
  • The bot may have a provision to push the ball only. No mechanism is to be present on the bot by which it may grab or hold the ball to itself.
  • On board power supply.
  • The game would be paused if any tangling of wires occurs which will be resolved by the referee.
  • The game would start and stop by the calls of the referee and his decision will be final and binding.
