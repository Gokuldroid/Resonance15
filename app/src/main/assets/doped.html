Get high on Electronics!

Description

Bored of living in this world? There is this world, "The world of Electronics" where Doping is no more a sin, but a boon. Get ready to immense yourself in this complex yet enthralling land of electronics. Practical knowledge about electronic devices and circuits combined with what you learned within the walls of the classroom is what you require to attain the ultimate triumph.

So let the frenzy, furor and fuss of the devices and circuits run through your veins and prepare yourself to get doped!

Rules & Format

  • The event consists of a single round of duration 45 minutes.
  • It is an individual event exclusively for second years
  • This is a pen and paper event. Use of calculators and other electronic gadgets are not allowed.
  • No negative marking.
  • Decision of the organizers is final.
